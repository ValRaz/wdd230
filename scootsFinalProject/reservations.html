Rental Agreement Information

  • A $50 security deposit is required to confirm all reservations.
  • A valid driver's license is required in hand at the time of rental.
  • No one under the age of 12 years of age is allowed to ride on the scooters.
  • Fuel charge is not included.
  • Unlimited mileage is included with all rentals.
  • Liability insurance is included with all rentals.
  • Damage insurance is available for purchase at the time of rental.
  • Free delivery to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs for rentals longer than two days.
  • We accept payment via Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or Venmo.
  • Rentals may be returned to either physical location.
